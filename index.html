Guest Information

  • Arrival Time: The doors to the museum open at 5:30pm, and guests are strictly not allowed in until then. The ceremeony will begin promptly at 6:00pm, so please arrive as closely to 5:30pm as possible to find your seat.
  • Parking: The museum has a 255-spot parking lot available that can accomodate all guests. Regardless, if you are staying in a nearby hotel, we recommend walking, and we always encourage carpooling. Additionally, The Joslyn is located conveniently near the ORBT Bus 24th Street stop.
  • Dinner & Drinks: A buffet-style dinner will be served during the reception, featuring a chicken taco bar with rice and beans. A cash bar will be available at the guest's cost with a selection of beer, wine, and signature cocktails. All payment types are accepted.
  • Hotels: For our out-of-town guests, we want to make your stay as convenient as possible. As part of the RSVP, we ask if you plan on staying in a hotel. Based on the number of guests who say yes, we will try to find a nearby hotel that can offer a discount or block of rooms.

Schedule

  • 5:30pm: Arrival. Doors open for guests. Please arrive as closely to this time as possible to find your seat.
  • 6:00 - 6:30pm: Ceremony. The ceremony will begin promptly at 6:00pm. Please be seated by this time.
  • 6:30 - 7:30pm: Cocktail Hour. Once the ceremony has concluded, guests will have access to the bar and art galleries for an hour. The newlyweds will take photos during this time. Please note no drinks are allowed in the galleries.
  • 7:30pm: Dinner. We will be serving a buffet-style dinner in the Fountain Court. Guests will be able to choose from a chicken taco bar with rice and beans.
  • 7:30 - 11:30pm: Reception. The reception will include dinner, cash bar, toasts, dancing, pictures, and more! We will have a DJ playing music throughout the night.
  • 11:30pm: Last Call. Last call for drinks will be at 11:30pm. We ask that all guests leave the venue by midnight.

Dress Code

The dress code for our wedding is Formal to Cocktail Attire. Think formal dresses, nicer cocktail dresses, dressy jumpsuits, traditional suits, jacket and tie, etc. We kindly ask no denim, shorts, hats, or white/ivory. If you are hesitant about a dress choice, you can never dress too nice!
